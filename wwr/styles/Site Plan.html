Overview

Purpose

To share a little bit of info about rafting in AZ and surrounding areas while providing info on business that does everything rafting.

Audience

The target audience is young(ish) and middle-aged adults who like to travel or be outdoors. Those people might live in the region already. Additionally, our audience would include travelers from out of town or adventurers. All of whom would be seeking at least some info on rafting in the area but also those whod like to rent or buy equipment or pay to go out as a group with a guide/instructor. The audience does not have a good source of info on rafting in the area and/or does not know where to find gear. They'd likely be using a mobile device but all forms of access are likely.
